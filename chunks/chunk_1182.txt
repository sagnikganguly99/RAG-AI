Reward Points
On every purchase made through a credit card, 
certain points are earned which can then be redeemed 
once they get accumulated. The redemption can take 
place in the form of shopping vouchers or discount 
coupons and sometimes redeemed in the form of cash 
too. The more the reward points offered on each 
spending, the better it is for customers. Those who 
are particularly aware about their grace period – and 
pay all dues within the allowed grace period with 
no interest charges applicable – should prefer credit 
cards with more reward points. They may not worry 
about the interest rate.
