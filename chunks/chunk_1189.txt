For Financially Naive Customers
Financially naive customers are not aware of the 
interest charges, do not bother to know the grace 
period or the billing cycle, and generally fall prey to 
the additional charges levied including interest, as 
payment is made after due date. They do not avail of 
the actual benefit of using short-time credit without 
having to pay any interest rate and, as a result, end up 
paying a huge amount as interest charges.
